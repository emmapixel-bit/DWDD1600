Top athletes in the world

This is a subjective list of some of the most dominant and influential athletes in the history of sports, compiled from various sports publications and records. There is no consensus on who the "greatest" of all time is due to differences in era, sport, and personal opinion.

  1. Athlete Sport Notable Achievement
    Michael Jordan Basketball 6 NBA Championships, 5 MVP Awards, and widely regarded as the greatest player in NBA history.
  2. Athlete Sport Notable Achievement
    Serena Williams Tennis 23 Grand Slam singles titles (most by any player in the Open Era) and 4 Olympic gold medals.
  3. Athlete Sport Notable Achievement
    Muhammad Ali Boxing 3-time World Heavyweight Champion and a global icon for his athletic prowess and activism.
  4. Athlete Sport Notable Achievement
    Simone Biles Gymnastics The most decorated American gymnast with 30 World Championship medals and 7 Olympic medals.
  5. Athlete Sport Notable Achievement
    Michael Phelps Swimming The most decorated Olympian of all time, with 28 medals, including 23 golds.
  6. Athlete Sport Notable Achievement
    Mia Hamm Soccer 2-time Women's World Cup champion and Olympic gold medalist who was a trailblazer for women's soccer.
  7. Athlete Sport Notable Achievement
    Wayne Gretzky Hockey Known as "The Great One," he holds numerous NHL records, including most career points and assists.
  8. Athlete Sport Notable Achievement
    Jackie Joyner-Kersee Track and Field Often called the greatest female athlete ever, winning three Olympic golds in the heptathlon and long jump.
  9. Athlete Sport Notable Achievement
    Pele Soccer Often regarded as the greatest male soccer player, winning three FIFA World Cups with Brazil.
  10. Athlete Sport Notable Achievement
    Babe Didrikson Zaharias Multi-sport Excelled in track and field and golf, winning multiple Olympic medals and 10 LPGA majors.
