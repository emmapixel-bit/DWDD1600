The Greatest Athletes of All Time

This is a subjective list of some of the most dominant and influential athletes in the history of sports, compiled from various sports publications and records. There is no consensus on who the "greatest" of all time is due to differences in era, sport, and personal opinion.

  1. Athlete Sport Notable Achievement
    Michael Jordan Basketball 6 NBA Championships, 5 MVP Awards, and widely regarded as the greatest player in NBA history. His influence extended beyond the court, redefining sports marketing and athletic dominance.
  2. Athlete Sport Notable Achievement
    Serena Williams Tennis 23 Grand Slam singles titles (most by any player in the Open Era) and 4 Olympic gold medals. A symbol of resilience and empowerment, she dominated the sport for over two decades.
  3. Athlete Sport Notable Achievement
    Muhammad Ali Boxing 3-time World Heavyweight Champion and a global icon for his athletic prowess and activism. Known as "The Greatest," his influence transcended boxing and helped shape social and political history.
  4. Athlete Sport Notable Achievement
    Simone Biles Gymnastics The most decorated American gymnast with 30 World Championship medals and 7 Olympic medals. She has redefined gymnastics with her power and precision while also championing mental health awareness.
  5. Athlete Sport Notable Achievement
    Michael Phelps Swimming The most decorated Olympian of all time, with 28 medals, including 23 golds. His performance in Beijing 2008, where he won eight gold medals, cemented his legacy.
  6. Athlete Sport Notable Achievement
    Mia Hamm Soccer 2-time Women's World Cup champion and Olympic gold medalist who was a trailblazer for women's soccer. She was instrumental in raising the profile of women's soccer globally.
  7. Athlete Sport Notable Achievement
    Wayne Gretzky Hockey Known as "The Great One," he holds numerous NHL records, including most career points and assists by a wide margin. He changed the way the game was played with his vision and skill.
  8. Athlete Sport Notable Achievement
    Jackie Joyner-Kersee Track and Field Often called the greatest female athlete ever, winning three Olympic golds in the heptathlon and long jump. She dominated her events for over a decade.
  9. Athlete Sport Notable Achievement
    Pele Soccer Often regarded as the greatest male soccer player, winning three FIFA World Cups with Brazil. He is credited with popularizing the sport in North America.
  10. Athlete Sport Notable Achievement
    Babe Didrikson Zaharias Multi-sport Excelled in track and field and golf, winning multiple Olympic medals and 10 LPGA majors. She was a pioneer for women's sports and is often considered one of the greatest athletes of all time.
